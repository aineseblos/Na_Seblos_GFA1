Wave For Water

The Waves for Water Organization Philippines is a non-profit institution wherein, the members establish systems that could collect and store rainwater in places where groundwater is absent. This organization contributes to providing free clean water for all residents. Summer is here and heatwaves are getting stronger causing the weather in the Philippines to be hotter which may cause dehydration and heat stroke to many citizens. Therefore, I chose to support and endorse this non-profit organization because I believe that access to clean water is very important to people especially this heated season.
